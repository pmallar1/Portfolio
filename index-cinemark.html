Cinemark Movie Club

Movie Club is the paid monthly membership tier of Cinemark Movie Rewards.

In addition to the benefits available for Movie Fan members, Movie Club members get one ticket per month that rolls over if unused and can be shared with friends or family, 20% off concessions every visit, and waived online fees.
